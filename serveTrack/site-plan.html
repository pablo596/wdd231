ServeTrack Site Plan

Site Name

ServeTrack – This name communicates the dual purpose of service and accountability. It is concise, easy to remember, and strongly aligned with the mission of the platform.

Site Purpose

ServeTrack is a service-sharing platform designed to promote acts of service. Users can submit and browse opportunities to serve in their community. The site encourages volunteerism, connection, and spiritual growth.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find service opportunities related to education?
  • How can I submit a service project that my ward is organizing?

Color Schema

Primary: #2980b9 – Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
Secondary: #f39c12 – Used for highlights, badges, and icons.

Typography

  • Segoe UI – Used for body text and general layout.
  • Georgia – Used for inspirational quotes and headings to add contrast.
